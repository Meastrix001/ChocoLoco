About us

ChocoLoco Company has been delighting chocolate lovers in the Pacific Northeast with hand dipped truffles, artisan chocolates and confectioneries since 2021. ChocoLoco Company has three retail locations and a production facility located in Gent, Belgium. Our products are sold by several grocery stores and gift shops including Market of Choice, Natural Grocers.
