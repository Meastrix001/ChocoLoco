Chocoloco Chique

The Chocoloco is an artisan chocolaterie, founded in 1992 by Thabisa, Nick, Toly and Renaldo, with stores in Leuven and Ghent. The Chocoloco develops chocolates for starred restaurants, received a mention in the Michelin Guide, and is hailed by the national and international press as the world's number 1.
